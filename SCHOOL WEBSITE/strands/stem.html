Strand Overview: STEM

The STEM strand is an academically rigorous track focusing on advanced mathematical concepts and scientific inquiry. It is designed to prepare students for the innovation-driven careers of the future.

Skills Developed

  • Scientific data analysis
  • Advanced mathematical computation
  • Experimental research design

Subject Highlights

  • Pre-Calculus & Basic Calculus
  • General Biology, Physics, & Chemistry

Strand Activities and Projects

The STEM strand challenges students with advanced laboratory experiments, robotics competitions, and mathematical research projects aimed at solving real-world problems.
