Strand Overview: GAS

The GAS (General Academic Strand) is designed for students who are exploring various fields of study. It provides a balanced curriculum that combines electives from different strands to prepare students for any college degree.

Skills Developed

  • Versatile academic writing
  • Holistic critical thinking

Subject Highlights

  • Disaster Readiness and Risk Reduction
  • Organization and Management

Strand Activities and Projects

The GAS strand offers a versatile curriculum for students still exploring their career paths, providing a balanced mix of humanities and sciences.
