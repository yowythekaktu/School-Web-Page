Strand Overview: ICT

The Information and Communications Technology (ICT) strand under the TVL Track is designed to equip students with specialized technical skills in computing, digital design, and systems management. This strand prepares learners for immediate industry employment, entrepreneurship, or further studies in Computer Science and IT.

Skills Developed

  • Computer Systems Servicing (CSS)
  • Software and Web Development
  • Database Management
  • Digital Illustration & Animation

Subject Highlights

  • Empowerment Technologies
  • Computer Programming (.NET/Java)
  • Hardware Troubleshooting & Networking
  • Technical Drafting

Strand Activities and Projects

The ICT strand immerses students in the digital world through hands-on coding marathons, network configuration labs, and professional graphic design projects.
