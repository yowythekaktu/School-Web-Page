Strand Overview: ABM

The ABM strand focuses on financial management, business operations, and corporate leadership. It prepares students for the competitive world of commerce.

Skills Developed

  • Financial literacy and bookkeeping
  • Strategic business planning

Subject Highlights

  • Fundamentals of Accountancy
  • Principles of Marketing

Strand Activities and Projects

The ABM strand brings business to life through school-wide entrepreneurship fairs, business simulation games, and financial auditing workshops.
