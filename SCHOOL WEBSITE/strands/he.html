Strand Overview: HE

The Home Economics strand provides hands-on training in culinary arts and hospitality. Students graduate with industry-standard skills eligible for TESDA NC II certifications.

🍳 Skills Developed

  • Professional culinary skills
  • Hospitality & service excellence
  • Kitchen safety & sanitation

🥧 Subject Highlights

  • Cookery & Bread and Pastry
  • Food and Beverage Services
  • Housekeeping Operations
