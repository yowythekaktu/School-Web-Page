Strand Overview: HUMSS

The HUMSS strand is designed for students curious about social structures and the human condition. It emphasizes critical reading, writing, and public speaking.

Skills Developed

  • Public speaking and debate
  • Social research and advocacy

Subject Highlights

  • Philippine Politics and Governance
  • Creative Non-Fiction

Strand Activities and Projects

The HUMSS strand focuses on developing eloquence and civic responsibility through mock trials, community immersion programs, and creative writing workshops.
