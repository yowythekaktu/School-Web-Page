Strand Overview: STEM

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand is an academically rigorous track focusing on advanced mathematical concepts and scientific inquiry. It is designed to prepare students for the innovation-driven careers of the future.

🧪 Skills Developed

  • Scientific data analysis
  • Advanced mathematical computation
  • Experimental research design
  • Logical & Critical Thinking

📐 Subject Highlights

  • Pre-Calculus & Basic Calculus
  • General Biology & Chemistry
  • General Physics 1 & 2
  • Capstone Research Project
