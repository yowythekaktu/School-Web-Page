Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for Grade 11 enrollment?

Original PSA Birth Certificate, Form 138 (Report Card), and Good Moral Certificate. (Additional: 2x2 ID Pictures)

Is there a tuition fee?

None. OSHS is a public senior high school providing free quality education under the Department of Education.

Do you accept transferees for Grade 12?

Yes, depending on slot availability and the strand alignment of your previous subjects.
